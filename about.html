About Me

I'm a caffeine addicted front-end developer based in beautiful San Luis Obispo, CA, designing and coding web applications focused on simplicity and user-friendliness to create a modern display for clients around the United States.

On the digital side, I use HTML, CSS, and Javascript to build sites that do not just look good on your desktop PC, but are also fully responsive. I also have experience leveraging the use of Wordpress (including plugins and themes) in order to create a seamless customer to client interaction.

In addition to web design and development work, I also enjoy dabbling in UI/UX for additional applications and graphic design.

When not online, I can be found playing video games (Destiny 2 and Hearthstone are current addicitions), watching a hockey/baseball game with my soon-to-be husband, Kyle, or snuggling with my two fur-children, Hanzo and Buster.
